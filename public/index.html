For Junior Devs

What is Code Cubs?

A platform to give junior devs the competitive edge they need, and employers the opportunity to find top entry-level talent. As a beginner, you are often competing with others that have over 5 years experience. With Code Cubs, you are given the opportunity to compete with peers on the same level.
Show off your skills, and build your experience with those who want to hire new developers.


For Employers

How does it work?

We have several interactive resumes just waiting for employers to search.
If you're looking to hire, simply add the technologies and tools you want in an employee, and we will match you with the best entry-level talent.
