For Junior Devs

What is Code Cubs?

A Platform to give junior devs the competitive edge they need, and employers the opportunity to find top junior dev talent. As a junior developer you are always competing with developers that have over 5 + experience, with Code Cubs you are given the opportunity to compete with others in same level as you.
Just create your interactive resume and let that speak for itself.


For Employers

How does it work?

We have several interactives resumes just waiting for employers to search for. Just add the technologies stacks you are looking for and based on the criteria you provide us with we will display those that match it.
